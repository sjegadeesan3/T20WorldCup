The Narendra Modi Stadium, formerly known as Sardar Patel Stadium, is the world's largest cricket stadium. Located in Motera, Ahmedabad, it was completely rebuilt and inaugurated in 2020. Today it hosts the grandest stage in T20 cricket — the ICC Men's T20 World Cup Final 2026.
